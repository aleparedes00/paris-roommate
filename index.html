📍 The apartment

The apartment is located in Trudaine, one of my favorite neighborhoods in Paris. The avenue is wide, lined with beautiful trees, and surrounded by cafés, bakeries, restaurants and bars. It's lively without feeling overwhelming.

The closest metro is Anvers, with Pigalle and Barbès-Rochechouart also within easy walking distance.

The apartment is 80 m² with two bedrooms located at opposite ends, which means we each have plenty of privacy and don't disturb one another when working, relaxing or sleeping.

Your bedroom (around 8 m²) overlooks a quiet interior courtyard, so you'll hardly hear any street noise. It's furnished with a double bed, a wardrobe and a Kallax shelving unit.

The rest of the apartment includes:

  • A bright living room with high ceilings, beautiful moldings and hardwood floors.
  • A fully equipped open kitchen with an oven, dishwasher and washing machine.
  • Plenty of storage throughout the apartment.
  • Separate bathroom and toilet (which makes busy mornings much easier!).

Although the apartment is on the first floor, the large double-glazed windows let in plenty of natural light.

There's also a small extra room that I occasionally use to host visiting friends or rent out for up to one week per month. The room is fully equipped for working from home, so whenever it's available you're welcome to use it as your office.

To keep things comfortable for everyone, we also have a cleaning lady once a week. I simply ask that we both keep the shared spaces tidy and organized between visits.
