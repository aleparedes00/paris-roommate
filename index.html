The apartment

The apartment is located near Avenue Trudaine, surrounded by cafés, bakeries, restaurants and bars.

It is an 80 m² apartment with two bedrooms located at opposite ends, giving both roommates privacy and quiet.

What the apartment includes

  • Bright living room with high ceilings and moldings
  • Original parquet flooring
  • Fully equipped open kitchen
  • Oven, dishwasher and washing machine
  • Separate bathroom and toilet
  • Weekly cleaning service

The apartment also has a small guest room equipped for working from home. You can use it as an office whenever it is available. I occasionally use this room to host visiting friends or rent it out, for like a week per month. I want to be transparent about this from the beginning so we can both feel comfortable with the arrangement.

The Roommate

Independent, friendly and considerate

I'm looking for an established working professional who genuinely enjoys shared living while valuing privacy and independence.

  • Keeps shared areas clean and organized

  • Values responsible consumption
